Learning Objectives

After completing this module student should be able to:

  • analyze the structure or chemical substances to make predictions about their solubility in various media
  • identify the intermolecular force responsible for solubility; and
  • make predictions about solubility resulting from acid–base reactions.

Pre-Lab

  1. Reading 3.1 - Non-covalent interactions and physical properties
  2. Complete the Pre-Lab assignment (table of physicochemical properties and risks assessment using RAMP framework).

In-Lab

  • Group discussion where we will walk through the stagest of the experiment and make predictions about solubility.
  • Carry out Experiment 2 (A–E)

Post-Lab

Complete post-lab homework assignment
